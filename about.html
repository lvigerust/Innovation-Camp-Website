Who are we?

Super Necklace is a company founded by three students with a love for tech and innovation.
We all have different skills, experiences and backgrounds which all adds up to make a great team.
Our vision is to be the market leader in solutions for tracking and monitoring distance during a pandemic like COVID-19.

History

Super Necklace was founded in August 2020 during an innovation camp hosted by our university.
We were given a task to propose an idea for a problem connected to the ongoing pandemic we all have experienced this past year.
With this problem we came up with Super Necklace and has since put all our time and energy into this company and product.

Future

It's hard to predict the future of Super Necklace and to decide which direction we would like for the company to take.
It's not easy being a startup but we fully believe in our product, and our only wish is for Super Necklace to blossom and grow further, even when this pandemic is under control or gone.
We hope you all will follow us on our journey!
