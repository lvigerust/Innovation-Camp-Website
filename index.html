Super Necklace

We all miss the days before the COVID-19 outbreak.
The Super Necklace is a product designed to limit the spread of COVID-19. Our goal is for everyone to be able to afford this product, so that we together can end this pandemic.

I'm interested

Affordable

Our product is made out of durable, yet cheap components. This makes us able to sell the Super Necklace at an affordable price.

Effective

The sensor inside will detect other necklaces' within a radius of 1.25m. It will make a beep sound within this radius to alarm the user.

Lightweight

We don't want the user to comprimise anything, so we figured out a solution to make the necklace barely noticeable when worn.

Made for safety

We want to reduce the spread of COVID-19 so less people get sick and more people can get back to work. One of the most effective ways to reduce spread is by keeping distance to eachother. The Super Necklace will help us with this.

Created for everyone

The potential of the Super Necklace is dependant on how many is using it. We therefore saw it crucial to make it available for everyone. The more people that are using the necklace, the more effectice it will be.
